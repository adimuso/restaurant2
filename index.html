The Suya Spot

Comfort food spot for Nigerian home cooking

The Suya Spot started with a gathering of friends who missed home cooking in the busy everyday lives. We missed the gathering spots we were used to visiting in Enugu and met once a month to recreate the atmosphere and share some soul warming home cooking.
